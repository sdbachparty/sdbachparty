BOAT PARTY
SAN DIEGO

By SDBachParty.com Team·Updated April 2025·8 min read

Ask any event planner, maid of honor, or best man who's organized a San Diego bach party what the highlight was — and the answer is almost always the boat. A private catamaran on San Diego Bay gives your group the Coronado Bridge at sunset, a full bar, your own playlist, and up to 100 guests celebrating together in one of the most spectacular settings in California. This is how you do a San Diego bach party properly.

01
Top Pick Up to 100 Guests

Triton Charters — San Diego's Best Party Boat

The largest and newest catamaran on San Diego Bay — built for exactly this kind of celebration.

Triton Charters operates San Diego's newest 75-foot luxury catamaran — the largest capacity boat on the bay — making it the go-to choice for bach party groups. The combination of size, amenities, and the crew's experience handling celebrations makes it the most popular choice for bachelorette and bachelor parties in San Diego.

The boat has everything a bach party needs: a 13-seat custom bar with cocktails, draft beer, wine, and spirits; a built-in waterslide off the back; a covered dance floor; professional Bluetooth sound system for your playlist; bean bag seating at the bow; and two restrooms onboard. The crew — captain, bartender, and deck crew — are experienced at making celebrations run smoothly.

For bach parties, Triton offers private charters that give your group exclusive use of the entire boat. You set the route (bay cruise, ocean sail, or both), bring your decorations, and the crew handles everything else. With capacity for up to 100 guests, it works for intimate groups of 20 and full squads of 80+.

Pricing is per hour based on group size — starting at $975/hour for up to 40 guests, scaling to $2,400/hour for up to 100 guests. Most bach party groups book 2-3 hours, which gives you the full bay circuit including the Coronado Bridge, the downtown skyline, and the Naval base. A 20% deposit secures the booking.

HOW TO BOOK TRITON CHARTERS FOR YOUR BACH PARTY

Book directly at triton-charters.com — private charters require a 20% deposit and a 3-week cancellation policy. The boat departs from 2700 Shelter Island Drive, San Diego CA 92106. Watch their pre-boarding video on YouTube for parking and arrival tips.

For summer weekend dates, book 6-8 weeks in advance. The most popular slots — Friday and Saturday evenings around sunset — sell out first. If your dates are flexible, Thursday evening charters often have better availability.

BACH BOAT PARTY TIPS
  • Book the boat 6-8 weeks in advance for summer weekend dates — this is non-negotiable for popular Saturday evening slots.
  • Ask about add-ons when booking — live music, custom decorations, catering coordination, and photographer arrangements are all possible with advance notice.
  • Arrive 20-30 minutes before departure to secure the best spots. On Triton, the bow bean bags and the upper deck seating fill up first.
  • Bring a light jacket — it gets cooler on the water in the evening even in summer, especially once you're moving.
  • The bar on private charters is included in the hourly rate — guests don't pay separately for drinks. Confirm this when booking.
  • Thursday evening charters often have better availability than Friday/Saturday and the sunset timing is identical.

BOAT PARTY SAN DIEGO FAQs

How much does a party boat charter cost in San Diego?
Private boat charters in San Diego start at around $975/hour for up to 40 guests, scaling up with group size. A 3-hour private charter for 40 guests runs approximately $2,925 total — split across the group, this is very reasonable for the experience. Ticketed shared cruises run $55 per person for 2.5 hours.
How many people can fit on a party boat in San Diego?
San Diego has party boats for groups of all sizes. Triton Charters operates the largest catamaran on the bay, accommodating up to 100 passengers on private charters. Smaller vessels accommodate 12-35 guests for more intimate group experiences.
How far in advance should I book a boat party in San Diego?
Book 6-8 weeks in advance for summer weekend dates. Spring and fall dates can typically be booked 3-4 weeks in advance. The boat charter is the one thing you should lock in first before planning any other elements of the weekend.
What is included in a private boat charter in San Diego?
Most private boat charters include a USCG certified captain and crew, a full bar with bartender, all safety equipment, and a sound system. Triton Charters also includes a waterslide, dance floor, and 2 restrooms on their 75-foot catamaran.
