PLAN YOUR TRIP

🗓️
When to Go
September through November is San Diego's golden window — warm evenings, clear skies, fewer crowds than peak summer. May through August is also excellent but book everything earlier.
 🏠
Where to Stay
Rent a group house in Mission Beach or Pacific Beach for the full party experience. Downtown hotels work well if you're doing Gaslamp nightlife. La Jolla for upscale groups.
 ⛵
Book the Boat First
Boat charters book out weeks in advance in summer — this is the one thing you cannot leave to the last minute. Lock in the boat, then plan everything else around it.
 💰
Budget Planning
Typical San Diego bach weekend: $400-$800 per person including accommodation, activities, and nights out. A private boat charter splits well across groups of 20+.
 🎉
Venues & Reservations
San Diego's best venues and restaurants require advance bookings for groups. Saturday nights in the Gaslamp fill up fast — reserve 3-4 weeks ahead for the best options.
 🌤️
San Diego Weather
266 sunny days a year and mild temperatures year-round. June has morning marine layer (June gloom) that clears by afternoon. Summers are warm but rarely hot. Perfect party weather.
GOT QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know about planning a bach party in San Diego — answered by people who've helped plan hundreds of them.

Start Planning
What is the best bachelorette party idea in San Diego?
A yacht or catamaran boat party on San Diego Bay is consistently voted the best bachelorette party experience in the city. The combination of the bay, the skyline, a private boat, and a full bar makes for an unforgettable celebration. San Diego also offers excellent bar crawls in the Gaslamp Quarter, beach clubs, rooftop bars, and spa days.
How much does a bachelorette party in San Diego cost?
A San Diego bachelorette party weekend typically costs $300-$800 per person depending on activities. A boat charter typically runs $975-$2,400/hour depending on group size. Bar crawls and restaurant experiences run $100-$200 per person per night. Accommodation in a group house or hotel adds $80-$200 per person per night.
When is the best time for a bachelorette party in San Diego?
September through November is the best time — weather is warm and consistent, the marine layer has cleared, and the city is slightly less crowded than peak summer. May through August is also excellent but book everything further in advance.
How far in advance should I book a San Diego bach party?
For summer weekends (June-August), book boat charters and group venues 6-8 weeks in advance. For fall and spring weekends, 3-4 weeks is usually sufficient. The boat charter and group accommodation are the two things to lock in first — everything else can be arranged later.
What neighborhoods are best for a bach party in San Diego?
The Gaslamp Quarter for nightlife — 16 blocks of bars and clubs in a beautiful historic setting. Pacific Beach for beach bars and a more casual vibe. Little Italy for upscale dinners and rooftop bars. Mission Beach for group house rentals with beach access.
