Our story

ABOUT SDBACHPARTY

SDBachParty.com is an independent guide to bachelorette and bachelor party planning in San Diego — built by people who have planned (and attended) more San Diego bach parties than we'd like to admit.

San Diego is one of America's genuinely great bach party destinations — but most of the planning guides online are either out of date, full of paid placements, or just repackaging the same ten suggestions. We built this site to be the resource we wished existed when we were doing the planning ourselves.

WHAT WE COVER

Everything worth doing for a San Diego bach party — boat charters, bar crawls, venues, nightlife, day activities, planning tips, and honest assessments of what's actually worth the money. We cover both bachelorette and bachelor parties because the city's options serve both equally well.

OUR APPROACH

We only recommend places and experiences we'd genuinely book for our own celebrations. We don't accept payment for positive coverage. We update our content when things change. If something we've written is wrong, email us and we'll fix it.
